    The Carpentries is a volunteer organisation teaching foundational coding and data science skills to researchers worldwide. It includes Software Carpentry, Data Carpentry, and Library Carpentry.

    Carpentry@UiO is supported by the University of Oslo (UiO) and the UiO library and offers Carpentry workshops to graduates, researchers, and other staff from all faculties and units to facilitate sharing and re-using of data. For more information, read Mission Statement and our Charter.
