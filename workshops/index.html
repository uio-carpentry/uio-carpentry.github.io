    Workshops #

    We organize workshops multiple times a year. The best place to learn more about our workshops or register for one of them is the University of Oslo Library’s Carpentry course catalogue or sign up UiO@Carpentry mailing list. Inbetween workshops we meet for study groups. To learn more about the study group go here.

    Upcoming Workshops #

    Carpentry Workshop: Databases and SQL

    6 April 2022, Kristine Bonnevies hus, Proteome 3627, Blindernveien 31, 0371 Oslo

    Do you want, or need, to learn how to work with databases for your research, but have little or no previous knowledge? Do you want to refresh your basic skills in SQLite? Or, do you want your team members to learn how to use and create databases? If yes, then this workshop is for you and/or your team! (more …)

    Carpentry@UiO Data Analysis and Visualization in R and Tidyverse (Novices)

    11 April 2022, Ole-Johan Dahls hus, Gaustadalléen 23B, 0373 Oslo

    Do you work with datasets regularly and want to learn how to work with them in R? Do you want to make nice publication-ready plots? Do you know base R already, but want to learn about what this Tidyverse thing is? Then this workshop is for you and/or your team! (more …)
